Coaching
The Sumptuous Vegan Coaching program supports aspiring and new vegans. Are you ready to transition to partial or full-time plant-based eating? Let me help you. Click here to learn more.
Sumptuous Visual Cookbook The Sumptuous Vegan Visual Cookbook offers cooking demos of delicious recipes from around the world! Each video will be your step-by-step guide to creating various dishes, from clean to comfort foods.
Workshops The Sumptuous Vegan monthly virtual workshops present attendees with the basics of what a vegan lifestyle looks like. New and aspiring vegans learn the real reason dairy isn't good for us, meat and cheese substitutes, the different ways of tapping into veganism, tips for flavorful food, and much more. Resources and support are offered whether or not the attendees decide to transition to a plant-based lifestyle.
Ebooks Transitioning to plant-based eating comes with numerous obstacles. Over the years, I've learned many things the hard way. I wrote these ebooks so you can dodge the issues I've learned how to beat!
